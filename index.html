About

Erin Reeves, the main gardener at Rocket Gardening, has been growing vegetables in urban spaces since 2010. She believes deeply in the power of local, organic gardening to empower people and their communities and reduce our impact on the planet.

Rocket Dog is our resident mascot, cheerleader, and inspiration for the name of our business.
